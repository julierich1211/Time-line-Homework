Time Management Organizing Made Fun 20 Life Hacks

    RISE AND SHINE

    GOOD MORNING SUNSHINE! Make your bed when you get out of it. That way you won't forget!

  • 09/24/14 06:45

    Get Dressed and Comb Hair

    Put on the clothes you laid out the night before, prior to going to bed. Comb your hair. Put on your shoes. Check yourself in the mirror, make sure your zipper is zipped, collar is turned down, and all clothes are right side out and on frontwards! (Parents with boys will understand why things like that need to be listed!)

    Breakfast

    Eat your breakfast and clean up your mess. Do not leave cereal in your bowl, RINSE your dishes!
